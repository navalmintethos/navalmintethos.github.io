About

The author introduces itself

1 minute 2021-01-22

I'm a computer nerd from Germany, currently working towards my post-graduate degree. This blog will contain my ramblings, thoughts on reality, rationalism, morale and what ever occupies my mind. Sometimes even short stories.

I will use this as a device to practice my writing skills and as a public but anonymous diary. The entries will be either in English or German depending on my mood and the nature of the post.

I will blog here under a pseudonym to free myself from mental blocks and fears that what I write here will come back to haunt me in the future. Please honor my wish to stay anonymous.
